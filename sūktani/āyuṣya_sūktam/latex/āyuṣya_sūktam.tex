īśāno devaḥ sa na āyu̍rdadhā̱tu̱ tasmai juhomi haviṣā̍ ghṛte̱na~\,॥\,1\,॥ \\
\vspace{0.5cm}
vibhrājamānaḥ sarira̍sya ma̱dhyā̱dro̱ca̱mā̱no gharmaruci̍rya ā॒gāt~| 
sa mṛtyu pāśānapanu̍dya gho̱rā̱nī̱hā̱yu̱ṣeṇo ghṛtama̍ttu de̱vaḥ~\,॥\,2\,॥ \\
\vspace{0.5cm}
brahma jyotirbrahma patnīṣu ga̱rbha̱ṁ ya̱mā̱da̱dhāt pururūpa̍ṁ jaya̱ntam~| 
suvarṇa raṁbha grahama̍rka̱ma̱rcya̱ṁ ta̱mā̱yu॒ṣe vardhayāmo̍ ghṛte̱na~\,॥\,3\,॥ \\
\vspace{0.5cm}
śrīyaṁ lakṣmīmaubalāmambikā̱ṁ gā̱ṁ ṣa̱ṣṭhīṁ ca yā̱mindra sene̎tyudā̱huḥ~| 
tāṁ vidyāṁ brahmayonigṁ̍ surū̱pā̱mi̱hā̱yu̱ṣe tarpayāmo̍ ghṛte̱na ~\,॥\,4\,॥ \\ 
\vspace{0.5cm}
dākṣāyaṇyaḥ sarva yonya̍ḥ sayo̱nya̱ḥ sa̱ha̱sra̱śo viśvarūpā̍ virū̱pāḥ~| 
sasūnavaḥ sapataya̍ḥ sayū̱thyā̱ ā̱yu̱ṣe̱ṇo ghṛtamida̍ṁ juṣa̱ntām ~\,॥\,5\,॥ \\
\vspace{0.5cm}
divyā gaṇā bahu rūpā̎ḥ purā̱ṇā̱ āyuṣchido naḥ pramathna̍ntu vī̱rān~| 
tebhyo juhomi bahudhā̍ ghṛte̱na̱ mā̱ na̱ḥ pra̱jāgṁ rīriṣo mo̍ta vī̱rān~\,॥\,6\,॥ \\ 
\vspace{0.5cm}

bhṛgūn sarpāgṁścāṅgirāso̍’tha sa̱rvā̱n ghṛ̱ta̱gṁ hu̱tvā svāyuṣyā mahayā̍ma śa॒śvat~\,॥\,8\,॥ \\
